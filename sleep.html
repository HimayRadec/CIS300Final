Sleep

Why Do We Sleep

During the sleeping cycle the body is able to restore organs, bones and tissues. It is during sleep that the majority of muscle growth happens. When working out a specific muscle the excercises causes the tissue to breakdown. With proper nutrition the muscle is repaired during sleep and improved. The importance of sleep CAN NOT be stressed enough. Lack of sleep leads to less recovery and energy. Which in turn affects your total effort exerted during a workout.
